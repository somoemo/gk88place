Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Cach Choi Blackjack

1. Luat choi Blackjack

Truoc khi bat dau choi, ban can phai hieu ro ve luat choi cua Blackjack. Dieu nay se giup ban co mot cai nhin tong quan ve tro choi va de dang ap dung nhung chien thuat cu the.

  • Tro choi Blackjack duoc choi voi mot bo bai gom 52 la.
  • Muc tieu cua tro choi la co duoc diem so gan bang hoac bang 21 hon nguoi chia bai.
  • Neu tong diem cua ban vuot qua 21, ban se thua ngay lap tuc.
  • Trong truong hop ca ban va nguoi chia bai deu co tong diem gan bang nhau, tro choi se ket thuc voi ket qua hoa (push).
  • Ban co the yeu cau them cac la bai cho den khi tong diem dat duoc la 21 hoac gan bang 21.
  • Neu ban co duoc mot cap la bai cung muc diem, ban co the chia tay (split) de tao thanh hai tay doc lap.
  • Neu ban co duoc mot quan A va mot quan 10 (J, Q, K), ban se co duoc diem so cao nhat va duoc goi la “Blackjack”.

2. Chien thuat choi Blackjack

Sau khi hieu ro luat choi, ban can phai co mot chien thuat cu the de co the choi Blackjack hieu qua. Duoi day la mot so chien thuat choi pho bien ma ban co the ap dung.



a. Diem so co ban

Diem so co ban la diem so toi thieu ma ban can phai co de khong bi thua tran. Neu tong diem cua ban nho hon diem so co ban, ban co the yeu cau them la bai mot cach tu tin ma khong can phai lo lang bi thua.

  • Neu tong diem cua ban tu 5 den 8, ban nen yeu cau them la bai.
  • Neu tong diem cua ban tu 9 den 11, ban co the xem xet yeu cau them la bai tuy thuoc vao la bai ma nguoi chia bai dang mo.
  • Neu tong diem cua ban la 12, ban nen yeu cau them la bai neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 6.
  • Neu tong diem cua ban la tu 13 den 16, ban nen yeu cau them la bai neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 6 hoac 10.
  • Neu tong diem cua ban la tu 17 den 20, ban co the xem xet dung lai tai day hoac yeu cau them la bai tuy thuoc vao la bai ma nguoi chia bai dang mo.

b. Chia tay (split)

Nhu da de cap o tren, neu ban co duoc mot cap la bai cung muc diem, ban co the chia tay de tao thanh hai tay doc lap. Dieu nay se tang kha nang chien thang cua ban neu ban co duoc hai bo bai tot.

  • Neu ban co mot cap 10 hoac quan ao (J, Q, K), ban nen giu nguyen cap bai nay.
  • Neu ban co mot cap 9, ban nen chia tay neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 9 hoac 10.
  • Neu ban co mot cap 8, ban nen chia tay neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 9 hoac A.
  • Neu ban co mot cap 7, ban nen chia tay neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 7 hoac 10.
  • Neu ban co mot cap 6, ban nen chia tay neu nguoi chia bai dang mo la bai co diem so tu 2 den 6 hoac 8 den 10.

3. Nhung dieu can luu y khi choi Blackjack

Ngoai viec ap dung cac chien thuat cu the, ban can phai luu y den mot so dieu quan trong khi choi Blackjack de co the tang co hoi chien thang cua minh.

  • Luon lua chon ban choi co ty le tra thuong cao nhat.
  • Khong nen dat cuoc qua nhieu tien vao mot van choi.
  • Tranh dat cuoc dua tren cam tinh.
  • Khong nen choi khi dang trong trang thai qua met moi hoac co tam ly khong tot.
  • Luon kiem tra lai luat choi va cac chien thuat truoc khi bat dau choi.
  • Tap trung va dua ra quyet dinh dua tren cac chien thuat da hoc.

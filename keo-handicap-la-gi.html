Gioi thieu ve keo handicap

Trong linh vuc ca cuoc truc tuyen, co rat nhieu loai keo khac nhau va mot trong so do la keo handicap. Day la mot khai niem quan trong ma bat ky ai muon tham gia ca cuoc cung can phai hieu ro. Vay keo handicap la gi? Chung ta cung tim hieu ve dinh nghia cung nhu cach tinh toan keo handicap qua bai viet duoi day.



Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: Keo Handicap La Gi

Dinh nghia keo handicap

Theo dung nghia den thi handicap co nghia la “suc nang”, tuy nhien trong linh vuc ca cuoc, handicap lai co y nghia hoan toan khac. Keo handicap la mot loai keo dac biet trong do mot doi bong duoc cong hoac tru diem so truoc khi tran dau bat dau. Muc dich cua viec nay la de tao ra su can bang giua hai doi bong co suc manh chenh lech, tu do lam cho tran dau tro nen hap dan hon va co hoi gianh chien thang cua ca hai doi la bang nhau.

Cach tinh toan keo handicap

De tinh toan keo handicap, chung ta can biet duoc suc manh thuc su cua hai doi bong doi dau. Sau do, mot so yeu to khac cung duoc xem xet nhu su phan bo luc luong, su thieu vang cua cac cau thu chu chot, phong do hien tai cua doi bong… Tu do, nha cai se quyet dinh cong hoac tru diem so cho mot trong hai doi bong de tao ra su can bang.



Vi du nhu tran dau giua doi A va doi B, nha cai du doan doi A co suc manh hon doi B, tuy nhien doi A lai bi thieu vang mot so cau thu chu chot. Keo handicap duoc dua ra la A -1/2 - B +1/2. Dieu nay co nghia la doi A se bi tru di 1/2 diem va doi B se duoc cong them 1/2 diem truoc khi tran dau bat dau. Neu ban dat cuoc vao doi A va doi A thang tran dau voi ti so 1-0, ban se thang keo neu cuoc vao doi B thi ban se thua keo.

Tai sao nen chon keo handicap

So voi cac loai keo khac nhu keo chau A hay keo chap, keo handicap co nhieu uu diem hon. Dau tien, keo handicap giup tao ra su can bang giua hai doi bong co suc manh chenh lech, tu do lam cho tran dau tro nen hap dan hon va co hoi gianh chien thang cua ca hai doi la bang nhau. Vi vay, neu ban dat cuoc vao doi yeu hon thi ti le thang cuoc se cao hon va nguoc lai.

Ngoai ra, keo handicap con giup nguoi choi dat cuoc co the kiem soat duoc rui ro. Boi vi khi biet duoc ti le va cach tinh toan keo handicap, ban co the du doan duoc ket qua cua tran dau mot cach chinh xac hon va dat cuoc theo dung keo ma ban muon.

Vi vay, neu ban muon thu van may va kiem them thu nhap tu viec ca cuoc truc tuyen, hay can nhac den keo handicap nhe.
